Apple Giant is a independent group all dedicated to makes lovable Pokemons plushies! We goal is to bring joy to the faces of children through our plush Pokémon toys.

Pokémon has a rich history that dates back to 1996 when the first game was released in Japan. Since then, the franchise has grown into a global phenomenon with video games, trading cards, movies, TV shows, and of course, plush toys. The Pokémon universe is vast and diverse, featuring over 800 different species of Pokémon, each with their unique personalities, skills, and quirks. Whether you're a long-time fan or new to the world of Pokémon, there's always something new and exciting to discover. And we're proud to be a part of this amazing community, bringing the joy and magic of Pokémon to fans of all ages through our plush lines.


Today is the special day we launch out very first product: Life-Size Appletun plusie! This sweet dragon from the Galar region has taken the Pokémon world by storm with its irresistible cuteness, but don't let its looks fool you! Appletun is a powerful Pokémon with impressive skills.
