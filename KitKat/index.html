KitKat's Story

1935 - The Original

Kitkat was first launched in UK named "Chocolate Crips", it even back them it had the famous four fingers.

1937-Hello KitKat

The now iconic name and brand logo of KitKat first graced out.

1944 - The First Variation

During the Second World War, KitKats were made using dark chocolate, and the packaging was blue rather than the iconic red.

1958 -Birth Of The Break

The famous "Have a break, have a KitKat" strapline was first used, it been with us ever since.

1960 - Two-fingers

Now a classic, the first two-finger KitKat was launched.

1999 - KitKat Chunky

The original Chunky was introduced and becomes an instant favorite.

2007 - KitKat Ice Cream

The first KitKat ice cream was launch, providing a perfect break for a sunny day.

2014 - KitKat Factory

The first KitKat Chocolatory was launched in Japan, allowing fans to design their own flavor of KitKat for the ultimate personalized break. Our Australian Chocolatory wasn't far behind

2016 - Sustainable Cocoa

KiKat becomes the first global chocolate brand to sus 100% sustainaby sourced Cocoa

2018 - The Ruby

The beautiful pink Ruby KitKat was launched.

2019 - A Seasonal Break

KitKat Christmas breaks are launched, along with Kitkat Cacao fruit.

2020 - 85th Anniversary

To mark the ocasion, we pledged to becomes carbon neutral by 2025.

2021 - Plant-based

The perfect balance of crispy wafer and smooth chocolate, now certified by the Vegan Society!
